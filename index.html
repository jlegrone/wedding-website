Emily & Jacob

We met in Charlotte's contra, swing, and blues dance scene in 2017. After dancing and chatting a few times, we went on a date and soon became inseparable. We have lived together in New York City for five years, and have loved and supported each other in several moves, job changes, new and sustained friendships, and the adoption of one rowdy but lovable dog. Transportation

If arriving by car, park in the main parking lot (in front of the Education Center) and follow signs to the Chapel of Peace. Please note that the driveway and parking lot at Hart Square are loosely packed gravel.

There will be a shuttle service running several times on Saturday between the wedding hotels and Hart Square. The shuttle to the venue will depart at 2:45 PM from the Courtyard by Marriott.

For transportation between the Charlotte airport and Hickory area hotels, reach out to Hickory Hop to reserve a shuttle ride. They run frequently throughout the day and offer a group discount if you book together. Reach out to Emily or Jacob for help coordinating with other guests.

What is Contra?

Contra is an American community folk dance. It is danced in long lines of partners, usually to live music. Contra is great for beginners because a caller guides the group through the steps.
